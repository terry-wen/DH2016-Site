Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend? +

If you're a college student and at least 18 years old, you're more than welcome to come participate!

Help! I can't connect to the Slack. +

You should have received an invite to our Slack via the email you signed up with. If you have not received an email, please message team@deserthacks.org and ask to be added!

How do teams work? +

Teams will consist of a max of 4 members. However, you are more than welcome to work by yourself, but we do encourage collaboration.

I'm stuck. What do I do? +

Worry not! We have many mentors on board who can help you with any issues you may run into. In addition, you can check out Skillflow, a Slack-integrated bot that can answer your coding questions 24/7.

What are the rules? +

How do I check out hardware? +

Hardware checkout is in the main lobby. You will need a government-issued ID to checkout hardware for the duration of the event.

How will I demo my hack? +

Hacks will be demo'd presentation style! Each team will have a couple minutes to show the audience and panel of judges what they've developed over the past 24 hours.
